About Us

Metabolic Reset for Techies is an AI-powered metabolic health platform designed specifically for desk-bound professionals. We understand the unique challenges faced by tech workers who spend long hours sitting at their desks, leading to metabolic decline, fatigue, and health issues.

Our platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with science-backed metabolic health principles to help you optimize your Non-Exercise Activity Thermogenesis (NEAT) without requiring gym time or workout schedules. We focus on practical, desk-friendly solutions that fit seamlessly into your busy tech lifestyle.
